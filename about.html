About Us

Malayalam Movies Subtitle Project is a non-profit initiative started by a group of friends. Our mission is to make the rich world of Malayalam cinema accessible to a global audience by providing high-quality English subtitles for classic films.

Many remarkable Malayalam films, especially older ones, lack accurate or sufficient English subtitles. This gap limits the ability of non-Malayali cinephiles to fully appreciate the unique storytelling, cultural nuances, and artistic brilliance of these films. Through our subtitling efforts, we aim to expand the reach of Malayalam cinema, introducing these films to a wider national and international audience.

We firmly stand against piracy and copyright infringement. Our initiative focuses solely on creating and providing English subtitles.

The goal of this project is to broaden the global reach of Malayalam films, and help preserve these cinematic gems for future generations.
